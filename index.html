        What is Y2K?
        
Y2K is where dead meme coins go to die with dignity. A brutalist dApp that gives abandoned tokens a final resting place on-chain. Pair your dead coin with Y2K and let it rot with meaning.

      

      

        How It Works
        
1. Submit your failed coin
2. Pair it with Y2K (LP)
3. It gets a tombstone on the Graveyard wall
4. Community decides if it deserves resurrection or eternal death.
